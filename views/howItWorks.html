ACCUMULATE POINTS ON YOUR PURCHASE

USSD: DIAL *120*960#

DISCOUNT VOUCHERS


MOBISITE & APP:

STEP 1:
ACCESS EITHER THE MAHALA APP OR BY BROWSING TO WWW.MAHALA.MOBI ON YOUR MOBILE DEVICE

STEP 2:
LOGIN USING YOUR MAHALA CREDENTIALS (FIRST TIME USERS CAN USE THEIR MAHALA LOYALTY NUMBER AS THEIR USERNAME AND PASSWORD)

STEP 3:
BROWSE TO THE DISCOUNT VOUCHERS TAB

STEP 4:
NOW YOU CAN EITHER SEARCH FOR A DISCOUNT VOUCHER BY SEARCHING BY PARTNER NAME, PARTNER CATEGORY OR BY PROVINCE

STEP 5:
DEPENDING ON YOUR SEARCH CRITERIA YOU WILL BE TAKEN TO A FULL LIST OF PARTNERS

STEP 6:
NOW JUST SELECT THE PARTNER THAT IS MOST CONVENIENT TO YOU AND YOU WILL RECEIVE THE RELEVANT DISCOUNT VOUCHER ON SCREEN

STEP 7:
SIMPLY DISPLAY THIS VOUCHER TO THE CASHIER AT THE CHECKOUT ALONG WITH YOUR MAHALA MEMBERSHIP CARD TO RECEIVE YOUR DISCOUNT!

USSD:

STEP 1:
ACCESS EITHER THE MAHALA USSD SERVICE BY DIALING *120*960# ON YOUR MOBILE DEVICE

STEP 2:
FOLLOW THE PROMPTS AND LOGIN USING YOUR MAHALA CREDENTIALS (FIRST TIME USERS CAN USE THEIR MAHALA LOYALTY NUMBER AS THEIR USERNAME AND PASSWORD)

STEP 3:
ENTER 3. TO SELECT DISCOUNT VOUCHERS

STEP 4:
NEXT ENTER THE NAME OF THE PARTNER YOU WISH TO USE

STEP 5:
YOU WILL RECEIVE AN SMS WITH YOUR DISCOUNT VOUCHER. SIMPLY DISPLAY THIS TO THE CASHIER ALONG WITH YOUR MAHALA MEMBERSHIP CARD WHEN YOU CHECK OUT
