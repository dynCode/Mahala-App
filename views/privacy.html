(Disclaimer to members of communities using the Mahala Mobile Platform and/or Mahala Loyalty Programme)

Protection of Personal Information:

The protection of client’s data, including personal information, is of critical importance to Mahala. Mahala takes appropriate measures to prevent and minimise risks of unauthorised access to, inappropriate use and the inaccuracy of the customer’s personal information. Mahala will not disclose the customer’s personal information to a person who is not directly involved in the delivery of our products/services without the customer’s permission, unless compelled by law/in terms of a court order to do so, or in public interest or necessary to protect the rights and ensure the integrity and operation of its business and systems. For more information, please view our Data Security Description.

Use of your personal information:

  1. Mahala collects your personal information for the following reasons:
    1. Name & Surname - to identify you on the database
    2. Identity number - unique identifier to assure we have no duplicates on the system
    3. Gender - to match applicable deals that are relevant to you
    4. Contact Numbers - to inform you of new deals available to you
    5. Address - to assist Mahala to negotiate deals within your region
    6. E-mail address - to inform you of new deals available to you
  2. Mahala will only communicate information relevant to Mahala Loyalty Programme and/or your benefits applied for within the prescribed format when you sign-up.

How to withdraw your consent:

You can withdraw your consent the following ways:

  1. Contact Mahala on 0860 624252, all calls are recorded for your and our protection.
  2. Send an e-mail to info@mahalas.co.za with your Name, Surname, ID number or loyalty number.
  3. Click on the “Unsubscribe” link shown at the bottom of each e-mail communication from Mahala.

Data Security Description

Communication from Mahala (Emails, E Notices, SMS’ etc)

  1. Mahala will only send communication to community members that have indicated at sign-up that Mahala can share information. Any communication received is sent by Mahala through our system providers named Loyaltyplus.
  2. Communication sent from Mahala is therefore not considered as bulk communication that can be classified as SPAM. Public “opt-out” databases are therefore not considered for the delivery of any communication via Mahala.
  3. If you wish to no longer receive communication from Mahala, you can unsubscribe or “opt-out” at any stage and Mahala will exclude you from any future communication. Please be aware that, in case you are removed from our communication list, you will not receive relevant news or notices, which could include e-wallet statements, new shops joining the platform etc.
