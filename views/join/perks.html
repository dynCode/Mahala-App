MAHALA LOYALTY BENEFITS

INCLUDED IN ALL PACKAGES

Your Mahala loyalty card brings you a WORLD of discounts and points at more than 1 000 Partners countrywide. Your loyalty card will be the key in accumulating points at Partners like Midas, Solo Shoes and many more.

You can access your discounts by down-loading a voucher on your phone (smart phones and basic phones).

Mahala Loyalty Club gives back to their members through a QuarterlyPrize draw. The prize money grows with the membership total of the Mahala Perks programme. For every 1000 members the prize money grows with R5000. That means if there are 10 000 members the prize pool will be R50 000 cash, payable to the lucky quarterly draw winner.

MEDICAL ADVICE

INCLUDED IN ALL PACKAGES

Medical personnel, including paramedics, nurses and doctors are available 24 hours a day to provide general medical information and advice. This is an advisory service, as a telephonic conversation does not permit an accurate diagnosis.

LEGAL ADVICE

INCLUDED IN ALL PACKAGES

Members and their immediate family have on-going access to a 24-hour legal advisory service such as criminal law, family law, constitutional law, child law, labour law, motor law, etc.

CRIME ASSIST

INCLUDED IN ALL PACKAGES

This is a 24 hour crisis management product to assist you in the event of a hijacking or home invasion. We will assist with the following:

Hijacking:
  • Prepaid airtime to the value of R100 and a cell phone when your cell phone is stolen.
  • Group B car hire for 48 hours.
Home Invasion:
  • Hotel accommodation to the value of R1000 per annum.
  • Security Guard at your residence for 24 hours.

MOTOR LAW

INCLUDED IN SILVER AND GOLD PACKAGES

Motor Law offers a comprehensive and powerful legal protection for motorists and passengers with a 24/7 call centre that is in service 365 days a year.

Some of the matters we can assist with:

  • RAF claims.
  • Legal representation:
    - High and Magistrate's court
  • Traffic offences above R1500 fines
  • Drunken driving
  • Bail applications
  • Recovery of accident damages to member's vehicle if not insured
  • Repossessions
  • Unlawful arrest by SAPS and Metro Police, etc

INTELLIGENT PANIC

INCLUDED IN SILVER AND GOLD PACKAGES

Intelligent Panic provides you and your loved ones with 24 hour access to your own experienced crisis manager – who will:

  • Help you through your emergency.
  • Call you back if needed.
  • Will be in telephonic contact until the crisis situation is resolved.

HOME ASSISTANCE

INCLUDED IN SILVER AND GOLD PACKAGES

In the event of a home emergency, we will arrange for an appropriate repairer (electrician, plumber, locksmith and glazier) to address the problem at one nominated address (call-out fee and first hour labour is covered, thereafter normal rates apply).

Please note that all parts and materials used are excluded and will be for the members account. Maintenance related issues are not covered. Overall limit of 3 incidents or up to R2 000 per annum per policy.

HOME SAFE CHAUFFEUR

INCLUDED IN THE GOLD PACKAGE

Statistics show car incidents as a result of drinking and driving account for a large percentage of accidents on our roads, especially at night. Home Safe Chauffeur will ensure that you and your vehicle arrive home safely. We will dispatch a vehicle with two drivers (where possible) and drive you home in your own car.

All our drivers:
  • Carry a valid Drivers Licence
  • Carry a cell phone
  • Dress professionally
  • Speak English

Service is restricted to 6 times per annum. Each incident is capped at R500.00, any costs incurred over and above this will be for the client’s account.

MISSING ASSIST

INCLUDED IN THE GOLD PACKAGE

What do you do when a loved one goes missing?

The People Search and Rescue Unit (PSARU™) is a 24/7 call centre help line specialized in providing holistic support in missing persons cases.

PSARU™ offer the following assistance:
  • 24/7 Call Centre for Immediate Assistance.
  • Specialized investigation team to be dispatched immediately in the search for a missing person.
  • Trauma Support.
